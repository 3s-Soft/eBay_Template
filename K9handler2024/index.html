📝 Item Description

Up for sale is a pair of Nike Air Flight 89 in the incredibly rare Cork / Black / Volt colourway. The Air Flight 89 is an iconic hi-top basketball silhouette — and this limited cork upper version is one of the hardest colourways to track down.

"The cork upper is genuinely stunning in person — every pair is unique thanks to the natural material. The volt green outsole makes this one of the most eye-catching limited Nike releases out there."

Originally released as a limited edition, the Air Flight 89 Cork features a full natural cork upper paired with a bold volt green midsole/outsole and clean black Swoosh branding. These are a true collector's piece and a standout addition to any rotation.

Photographed in natural light exactly as they are. Pre-owned with visible wear but plenty of life left in them. A rare opportunity to own a very limited Nike colourway.
