Important Information

Care & Maintenance Guidelines

Clean your quartz stone surfaces regularly with soapy water or a neutral mild detergent and a soft cloth/sponge. Rinse off surfaces with clean water. Polish dry with a soft cloth.

DO NOT use abrasive cleaning products on your Quartz slabs. Bleach, chlorine-based cleaners, acids, alkalis or concentrated disinfectants will damage your stone surfaces.

Always use pan stands and trivets to protect stone surfaces from hot and rough pans and utensils. Quartz is heat resistant NOT heatproof.

Always use cutting boards to chop and slice food. Never cut directly onto the stone surfaces. Quartz is scratch-resistant NOT scratch-proof.

For stubborn stains, use recommended products such as Cif Original Cream or The Pink Stuff with a microfibre cloth. Do not use bleach or acidic products.

GMQ Stone Slabs are designed for indoor use only. Using the slabs outdoors will eventually lead to discolouration and this is not covered by your warranty.
